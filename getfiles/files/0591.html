Half Plug is an online publisher of Print and Play games.

You can donwnload Board Games, Card Games and Role Playing Games from thier website (see the link above) for free.
But there is also a link on every game page that allows you to donate any amount either to the designer of the game or to a charity organization.

They also offer a chat service that allows users to play their favorite RPG's with new groups located anywhere through their 6 on 6 video chat.
